A 2D Space Invader Game

Earth has been invaded and it is up to you to save it!
Invasion brings you, the player, the ultimate experience with custom sounds and animations.

Fight Through Waves of Enemies

You have the opportunity to level up and become more powerful the higher your score, but be warned, the enemies can do the same thing. Can you make it higher than wave 20 without being defeated?

Try to Beat Your Score

Invasion will provide you with your score at the end of every game. Try to beat your score each time and save the Earth from Invasion.
