A Whiteboard App

This app allows you to write, paint, draw whatever you can think of. With multiple colors to choose from.

Save It

Say you want to save your creation by taking a picture of it. No problem, just click the frame in the top right corner and take a screenshot without the bottom bar blocking your way.

Make A Masterpiece

Inspired to paint then make a masterpiece. You have the tools at your disposal.

Change Settings At Will

Play with multiple setting to get the perfect color, stroke thickness, and translucency.

Scrap It

Click the undo button to erase the last thing you did or if your still unhappy you can delete everything by clicking the trash can.
