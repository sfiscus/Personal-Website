A Tip Calculator App

Ever get the bill for a meal and wish you knew the right amount to leave for a tip? Me too! So let Bill Tipper find out for you.

Enter The Bill Amount

Simply enter the bill amount and the tip is calculated instantly.

Leave What You Want

If you want to leave more or less just use the custom tip option. This allows you to enter whatever percentage you want and the rest is done for you.

Slide It

The custom tip is easy to use with Slide Bar funcationality.

Oops Wrong Amount

If you make a mistake no worries just hit the reset values button and everything is reset like it never happened.
